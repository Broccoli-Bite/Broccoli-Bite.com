Broccoli Bites with Tahini Dipping
Sauce

14 December, 2023 7.3K
  • Twitter
  • Facebook

These flavorful BroccoliBites make for a delightful and healthy vegan dish. The combination of broccoli, quinoa, and spices gives them a satisfying texture and taste. Enjoy these as a snack, appetizer, or as part of a meal!

Ingredients:

For BroccoliBites:

  • 2 cups broccoli florets, steamed and finely chopped
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup chickpea flour
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

    • For Tahini Dipping Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt to taste

    • Instructions:

    Prepare the BroccoliBites:

  • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  • In a mixing bowl, combine the chopped broccoli, cooked quinoa, chickpea flour, nutritional yeast, minced garlic, smoked paprika, ground cumin, salt, and pepper.
  • Mix until well combined.
  • Form the mixture into small bite-sized patties or balls and place them onto the prepared baking sheet.
  • Drizzle the olive oil over the BroccoliBites.
  • Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown and crispy.

    • Prepare the Tahini Dipping Sauce:

  • In a small bowl, whisk together the tahini, lemon juice, maple syrup, water, ground cumin, and salt until smooth and well combined.
  • Adjust the consistency by adding more water if needed.
  • Once the BroccoliBites are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.
  • Arrange the BroccoliBites on a serving platter and serve warm alongside the prepared Tahini Dipping Sauce.
