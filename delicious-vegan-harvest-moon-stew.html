Delicious Vegan Harvest Moon Stew

Sitemap 100K
  • Twitter
  • Facebook

This Harvest Moon Stew is a comforting and hearty vegan meal that brings together the earthy richness of black rice and chickpeas with the vibrant flavors of seasonal vegetables. The addition of herb-infused dumplings provides a delightful twist, making each bite a celebration of taste and texture. Enjoy this unique culinary experience!

Ingredients

For The Stew Base:

  • 1 cup black rice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 1 cup diced butternut squash
  • 1 cup chopped kale
  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the Herb Dumplings
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk

    • Instructions:

    Prepare the Stew Base:

  • Rinse the black rice under cold water. Set aside.
  • Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add chopped onion and saut√© until translucent. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  • Stir in diced carrots, celery, butternut squash, and chopped kale. Cook for 5-7 minutes until vegetables start to soften.
  • Add drained chickpeas, black rice, vegetable broth, smoked paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30-35 minutes until the rice and vegetables are tender.

    • Prepare the Herb Dumplings:

  • In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, baking powder, salt, chopped parsley, and chopped chives.
  • Slowly add the unsweetened almond milk to the dry ingredients and mix until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

    • Assemble the Dumplings:

  • Once the stew is simmering and the rice and vegetables are cooked, use a spoon to drop small dollops of the herb dumpling batter on top of the simmering stew.
  • Cover the pot and let the dumplings cook for 15-20 minutes. They will puff up and become light and fluffy.

    • Serve:

  • Ladle the Harvest Moon Stew into bowls, ensuring each serving has a generous portion of vegetables, rice, chickpeas, and flavorful herb dumplings.
