Savory Coconut Curry Cabbage Rolls

Sitemap 910K
  • Twitter
  • Facebook

These unique and flavorful cabbage rolls, filled with a delightful chickpea-quinoa mixture and bathed in a creamy coconut curry sauce, create a dish that tantalizes the taste buds with every bite. Enjoy this innovative vegan meal that's sure to impress!

    Ingredients:

    For the Cabbage Rolls:

  • 1 large head of cabbage
  • 1 cup quinoa, cooked
  • 1 can (14 oz) chickpeas, drained and mashed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

    • For the Coconut Curry Sauce:

  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

    • Instructions:

    Prepare the Cabbage Leaves:

  • Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
  • Carefully remove the core from the cabbage and place the whole head into the boiling water.
  • Cook for 5-7 minutes or until the outer leaves are tender and pliable.
  • Remove the cabbage from the water and let it cool slightly.
  • Peel off the leaves carefully without tearing them.

    • Prepare the Filling:

  • In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until translucent.
  • Add the mashed chickpeas, cooked quinoa, curry powder, ground cumin, turmeric, salt, and pepper.
  • Cook for a few minutes until the flavors combine. Remove from heat and let it cool.

    • Assemble the Cabbage Rolls:

  • Take a cabbage leaf and place a spoonful of the chickpea-quinoa filling in the center.
  • Fold the sides of the leaf over the filling and roll it up, tucking the ends in to secure the filling. Repeat with the remaining leaves and filling.

    • Prepare the Coconut Curry Sauce:

  • In a saucepan, combine coconut milk, tomato paste, curry powder, garam masala, grated ginger, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  • Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring until well combined and heated through.

    • Cook the Cabbage Rolls:

  • Pour a small amount of the coconut curry sauce into a baking dish, spreading it evenly on the bottom.
  • Place the rolled cabbage leaves seam-side down in the baking dish.
  • Pour the remaining coconut curry sauce over the cabbage rolls, ensuring they are well covered.

    • Bake:

  • Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 minutes, or until the cabbage rolls are tender and the flavors meld together.

    • Serve:

  • Carefully remove the cabbage rolls from the oven.
  • Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving these delectable Savory Coconut Curry Cabbage Rolls.
