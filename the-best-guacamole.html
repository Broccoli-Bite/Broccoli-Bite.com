The BEST Guacamole

Sitemap 801K
  • Twitter
  • Facebook

    Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1-2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeño or serrano pepper, seeded and finely chopped (optional, for heat)
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1-2 limes (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Instructions:

  • Cut the avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a mixing bowl.
  • Mash the avocado with a fork or potato masher until it reaches your desired consistency (some prefer chunky guacamole, while others like it smoother).
  • Add the diced tomato, chopped red onion, minced garlic, jalapeño or serrano pepper (if using), and fresh cilantro to the mashed avocado.
  • Squeeze the lime juice over the mixture.
  • Start with the juice of one lime and add more if needed.
  • The lime not only adds flavor but also helps prevent the avocado from browning.
  • Season the guacamole with salt and pepper according to your taste preferences.
  • Mix everything together gently until well combined.
  • Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.
  • You might want to add more lime juice, salt, or pepper to suit your taste.
  • Serve immediately with tortilla chips, as a topping for tacos or nachos, or alongside your favorite Mexican dishes!
  • Remember, guacamole is best enjoyed fresh, so try to make it just before serving.
  • If you need to store it, press plastic wrap directly onto the surface of the guacamole to minimize air exposure and prevent browning.
  • Enjoy your homemade guacamole with a tasty tomato twist!
