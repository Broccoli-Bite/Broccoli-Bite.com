The Enchanted Garden Pizza

Sitemap 321K
  • Twitter
  • Facebook

This Enchanted Garden Pizza combines sweet, savory, and nutty elements for a taste that's truly out-of-this-world!

Ingredients:

For the Crust:

  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt

    • For the Toppings:

  • 1 cup roasted sweet potato cubes
  • 1 cup sliced portobello mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup sliced figs
  • 1/2 cup caramelized onions
  • 1/2 cup baby spinach leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped pistachios
  • Vegan mozzarella cheese (as desired)
  • Balsamic glaze (for drizzling)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • For the Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup tahini
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Instructions:

    Prepare the Dough:

  • Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Allow it to sit for 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  • In a mixing bowl, combine flour, salt, and olive oil. Pour in the yeast mixture and knead until a smooth dough forms.
  • Let it rest and rise in a warm place for about an hour.

    • Prepare the Sauce:

  • Whisk together tahini, minced garlic, lemon juice, water, maple syrup, salt, and pepper until smooth.
  • Adjust consistency with water if needed. Set aside.

    • Prep the Toppings:

  • Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  • Toss the sweet potato cubes and portobello mushrooms in olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  • Roast in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until tender.

    • Assemble the Pizza:

  • Roll out the risen dough on a floured surface to your preferred thickness.
  • Spread the tahini sauce evenly over the dough.
  • Layer the roasted sweet potatoes, portobello mushrooms, sliced figs, caramelized onions, baby spinach leaves, chopped pistachios, and vegan mozzarella cheese on top.
  • Place the assembled pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the toppings are cooked.
  • Once out of the oven, drizzle the pizza with balsamic glaze for a touch of sweetness.
  • Allow it to cool for a minute before slicing, then serve and savor the unique, enchanting flavors!
