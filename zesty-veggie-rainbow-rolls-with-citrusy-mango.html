Zesty Veggie Rainbow Rolls with Citrusy Mango

SITEMAP 4.4M
  • Twitter
  • Facebook

These rainbow rolls are not only visually appealing but also packed with various nutrients from the assortment of vegetables and fruits. The mango dipping sauce adds a delightful tanginess to complement the freshness of the rolls. Enjoy this colorful and healthy dish as a satisfying meal or snack while aiming for weight loss!

    Ingredients:

    For the Rainbow Rolls:

    8-10 rice paper wrappers

  • 1 cup shredded purple cabbage
  • 1 cup julienned carrots
  • 1 cup thinly sliced red bell peppers
  • 1 cup sliced cucumbers
  • 1 cup chopped lettuce or mixed greens
  • 1 ripe mango, thinly sliced
  • 1 avocado, thinly sliced
  • Fresh mint leaves and cilantro for garnish
  • Sesame seeds (optional)

    • For the Citrusy Mango Dipping Sauce:

  • 1 ripe mango, peeled and diced
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Zest of 1 lime
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon agave syrup or maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • Pinch of salt

    • Instructions:

  • Prepare all the veggies and fruits, ensuring they are thinly sliced and ready for rolling.
  • Fill a shallow pan or large plate with warm water. Dip one rice paper wrapper in the water for about 10-15 seconds until it softens.
  • Carefully transfer it to a clean, damp kitchen towel.
  • Assemble the rainbow rolls by placing a small amount of each vegetable and fruit in the center of the wrapper, leaving space at the sides.
  • Add a few mint and cilantro leaves.
  • Fold the sides of the wrapper towards the center, then roll it tightly from the bottom, sealing the fillings inside. Repeat with the remaining wrappers and fillings.
  • For the dipping sauce, blend together the diced mango, lime juice, lime zest, rice vinegar, agave syrup, grated ginger, and a pinch of salt until smooth.
  • Serve the rainbow rolls with the citrusy mango dipping sauce on the side. Garnish with sesame seeds if desired.
